Directions from University of Miami to Indian Creek

  1. Take Granada Blvd to US-1 N 43 s (246 ft)
  2. Follow US-1 N, I-95 N and I-195 E to FL-907 in Miami Beach. Take exit 5 from I-195 E 21 min (13.5 mi)
  3. Follow FL-907, Florida A1A N/Collins Ave and Indian Creek Island Rd to your destination in Indian Creek 22 min (6.8 mi)

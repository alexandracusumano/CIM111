Activies

On Indian Creek Island, one can enjoy themselves at the Indian Creek Country Club, playing golf or dining. There are 41 waterfront residencies where one can dock a boat. One can also take a visit to the famous Village Hall.

Food

One can dine with a membership to Indian Creek Country Club. There are a variety of restaurants over the bridge in South Beach as well.

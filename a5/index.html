How to Create a Flawless Complexion (Makeup Tutorial)


Keep your face looking perfect and matte all day long.

Follow these steps

Cleanse your face and apply a toner and moisturizer.

Take a foundation brush and evenly apply foundation to the skin. Blend with a beauty blender.

Take a concealer lighter than your foundation and apply under eyes and on blemishes. Blend with a beauty blender.

Set with a translucent pressed powder.

Set with your favorite setting spray.
